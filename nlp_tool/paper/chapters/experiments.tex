\section{Experimental Setup}

As a test bench, three word sets of English loan words adapted into Filipino
were created with their corresponding nativized form.

The first set consists of 100 random drug names taken from the internet. The
second set consists of 262 random drug names taken from the Philippines FDA
list of approved human.
drugs~\footnote{\url{https://verification.fda.gov.ph/drug_productslist.php}} The
third set consists of 100 random English words commonly used in Filipino
sentences or conversations, the words in this list were taken from random posts
on the r/Tagalog subreddit.~\footnote{\url{https://www.reddit.com/r/Tagalog}}
