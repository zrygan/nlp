diad Macpela a bokig na Mamre, diad dalin na Canaan. Sinaliw nen laki yon Abraham iman ya ongib tan alog ed kinen Efron a Heteo, pian pamponponan.
Diman so angipomponan dad kindi laki Abraham tan di asawa ran Sara; diman so angipomponan ed kindi laki Isaac tan di asawa ran Rebeca; tan diman met so angipomponan kod kinen Lea.
Asaliw ed saray Heteo ira may alog tan ongib. Diman yo ak ipompon.
