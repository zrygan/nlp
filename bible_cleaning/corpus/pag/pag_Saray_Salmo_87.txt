1: Inletneg na Katawan so siudad to diad tapew na masanton palandey;
2: inaro to niy siudad na Jerusalem nen say dinan man a pasen ed Israel.
3: Dengel mo, siudad na Dios,
: iray makapakelaw a bengatlan ibabaga ton nipaakar ed sika
4: No ilistak iray bansan ontotoluk ed siak,
: ipilak so Egipto tan say Babilonia;
: ipibiang ko a kabyangan na saray totood Jerusalem iray taga Filistia, Tiro, tan Etiopia.
5: Nipaakar ed Sion, nibaga a sakop ton amin iray bansa,
: tan pabiskegen na Makapanyarin-amin.
6: Say Katawan ilista to ray katoowan,
: tan ibilang to ran amin a mibabaley ed Jerusalem.
7: Onsasayaw tan ikakanta ra,
: Diad Sion so panlalapwan na amin a bindision tayo.
