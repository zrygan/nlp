1: Katawan , ontatangay ak ed sika,
: ditad tawen a panooleyan mo.
2: No panoy paniilalo na aripen ed amo ton laki,
: o say aripen a bii ed amo ton bii,
: ontan met so paniilalo mid sika,
: Katawan a Dios mi, anggad kasian mo kami.
3: Kasian mo kami pa, Katawan , kasian mo kami;
: ontan lay impakababalaw mi!
4: Abayag lay impanlelemlemew ed sikami na saray mamayaman,
: tan say impanbabalaw na saray maruksan mapaatagey.
