1: Ding taung magtiwala king Ginu kalupa re ing Bunduk ning Zion
: a kapilan man e matugun at midusug.
2: Nung makananu lang makapadurut ding bunduk king Jerusalem,
: makanyan neng padurutnan ning Ginu ing keyang balen ngeni at king kapilan man.
3: Uling ding e mayap e no man mamahala kabang-kaba king bansa ding makatuliran;
: miyawa la namang gawa king e mayap.
4: Ginu , kayapan ing ibie mu karing taung mayap a daraptan,
: karetang tapat karing kekang kautusan.
5: Potang parusan mu la ding taung e mayap,
: iyabe mu la ding painggulut karing kekang kautusan.
: Ing kapayapan manatili king Israel!
