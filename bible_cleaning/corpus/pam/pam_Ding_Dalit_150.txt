1: Purian ya ing Ginu !
: Purian ye ing Dios king Templo na!
: Purian yu ing sikanan na karin banua.
2: Purian ye uli ding mayupaya nang dapat;
: purian ye uling sakdal ya degulan!
3: Purian ye king tiup ding trumpeta;
: purian ye kayabe ding alpa at lira!
4: Purian ye kayabe ding tambul at king terak; purian ye kayabe ding alpa at plauta!
5: Purian ye kayabe ding pandereta;
: purian ye kayabe ding kakalaksing a pandereta!
6: Purian ye ing Ginu , ikong sablang melalang!
: Purian ya ing Ginu !
