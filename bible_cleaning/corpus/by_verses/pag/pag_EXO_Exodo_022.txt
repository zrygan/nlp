No say sakey a too et manakew na sakey a baka odino sakey a karniro tan pateyen to odino ilako to, nepeg to a bayaran na limaran baka so sakey a baka tan apatiran karniro so sakey a karniro.
Nepeg ton bayaran so tinakew to. No anggapon balut so kayaryan to, nepeg ya ilako a sakey ya aripen pian nabayaran so tinakew to. No nalmo ed sikato a mabilay so tinakew to a baka odino asno odino karniro, nepeg to a bayaran na duara so sakey.
No diad labi et nasiwa so sakey a matakew ya onloloob ed sakey ya abung tan apatey, say akapatey ed sikato et ag mankasalanan na impamatey. Balet no diad agew so kagawa to, mankasalanan na impamatey.
No say sakey a too et ipastol to ray ayep to ed alog odino diad kaubasan, et akaarawi ra tan kinan da ray bunga na tanaman ed alog na sakey a too, say akanayep nepeg ton bayaran so kinen na ayep to na saray sankaabigan a bungay tanaman ed kaalogan to odino kaubasan to.
No say sakey a too et mamool ed alog to tan onkayamkam so apoy ed alog na arum a too tan napoolan iray ontutubon tanaman diman odino saray pinalot a nisimbun diman, say angigapoy apoy et nepeg a bayaran toy adarak.
No say sakey a too et mipaknaan ya isinup toy kuarta odino antokaman a mablin kayaryan na sakey a too, insan natakew ira ed abung to, say nantakew nepeg a dobli so ibayar to no narel.
Balet no ag narel so nantakew, say toon angisinup na kayaryan et nepeg ya iyakar ed pasen a panangigalangan tan diman mansamba ya ag to tinakew so kayaryan na toon akipaknaan to.
Diad balang kolkolan a nipaakar ed pankayaryan na bengatlan, singa bilang baka, asno, karniro, kawes, odino antokaman ya abalang, saray duaran totoon mangakayaryan et nepeg iran niyakar ed pasen a panangigalangan. Say toon ibawag na Dios a sikatoy nankasalanan et nepeg a mambayar na dobli ed samay sakey a too.
No say sakey a too et mipaknaan ya asikasoen toy asno, baka, karniro odino arum ya ayep na sananey a too, tan samay ayep et inatey odino asugatan odino imbatik na matatakew, tan anggapoy makatasi ed inkagawa,
samay too nepeg ya onlad pasen a panangigalangan tan sambaan to ya ag to tinakew imay ayep na sakey a too. No ag atakew, say akanayep nepeg ton akoen a naandi, tan samay sakey a too et ag la nepeg a mambayar;
balet no atakew so ayep, samay too nepeg a bayaran to so akanayep.
No pinatey na saray ayep ed takel, say toon manaasikaso alaen to so laman na ayep a pamaneknek ed agawa; ag to nepeg a bayaran ed akanayep so pinatey na saray ayep ed takel.
No say sakey a too et ombayes na sakey ya ayep ed sakey a too tan asugatan odino inatey imay ayep nen andid man so akanayep, say toon binmayes et nepeg a bayaran to may binayes to.
Balet no say akanayep et wadman nen abalang so ayep, say binmayes ag nepeg a mambayar. No inabangan ya ayep, say abang et pinagkabayar la.
No say sakey a laki et tuksoen toy sakey a marikit ya ag ni akisipanan a mikasal tan akdulan to, say laki nepeg ya iter toy bayar a para nobia tan asawaen to may bii.
Balet no ag onabuloy so ama ya asawaen day anak to, samay laki nepeg a bayaran to may ama na singa karakel na kuartan ibayar ed nobian marikit.
Nepeg a pateyen so siopaman a biin managsalamangka diad pananulong na maoges ya ispiritu.
Nepeg a pateyen so siopaman a lakin misiping ed ayep.
Nepeg a pateyen so siopaman a mangibagat ed anggan anton dios likud ed siak a Katawan .
Ag yo lulukoen odino papairapen so sangkaili; ag yo lilingwanan a sangkaili kayo met nen saman ed Egipto.
Ag yo papairapen so siopaman a balon bii odino siopaman ya olila.
No pairapen yo ra, siak a Katawan ebatan ko ra no onkerew irad siak na tulong,
tan mampasnuk ak tan pateyen ta kayo ed bakal. Nabalo ray kaasawaan yo tan naolila ed ama iray anak yo.
No mampabayes kayo na kuarta ed siopaman a took a mairap so bilay to, ag yo ipapasey sikayon dili a managpaabang tan ag yo pangaabangen.
No alaen yoy kawes na sakey a too a sikatoy kaletan a bayaran to kayo, nepeg yon ipawil kasakbayan ya onselek so agew,
lapud sikato labat so nayarian ton ikawes a pamapetang na laman to. Anto niy arum a kawes a nayarian ton iyogip? No onkerew ed siak na tulong, ebatan ko lapud mapangasi ak.
Ag kayo mansasalita na maoges sumpad Dios, tan ag yo isasamba so pangulo na saray totoo yo.
Iter yod siak, no nasabi lay nigetar a panaon, iray bagat yon trigo, alak, tan saray larak na oliba.
Iter yod siak iray pangulowan ya anak yon lalaki.
Iter yod siak iray besag ya anak na baka tan karniro yo. Piamung yon pitoran agew ed ina to so besag a kalakyan, insan yo ibagat ed siak no kumawalon agew.
Sikayo ray totook, kanyan ag yo kakanen so karni na dinanman ya ayep a pinatey na saray ayep ed takel; iter yo lad saray aso.
